My marketing Journey

My marketing journey began back in 2010, when I started a Bachelor’s degree in Advertising and Social Communication at Diego Portales University in Chile — one of the country’s top-ranked universities in advertising. After graduating in 2014, I dove straight into the marketing world, gaining hands-on experience across the tourism industry and later at a boutique marketing agency, where I managed up to six brands at once. This fast-paced environment sharpened my skills in:

  • Social media marketing
  • Strategic planning
  • WordPress management
  • Community engagement
  • Graphic design
  • Photography

My Work in Australia

Today, I work at Satellite Foundation, a not-for-profit organisation that supports young people navigating mental health challenges. My role includes: Creating engaging content for social media Updating landing pages and managing our website Running email campaigns Optimising internal workflows and marketing strategy In addition to my role at Satellite, I’ve worked with several other Australian organisations, including: Mister Magnets – promotional marketing IGS – product marketing and design BCTA – marketing support for the construction training sector

My Passion Project

Casa Peak Grampians

Outside of my 9–5, I manage Casa Peak Grampians, a cozy Airbnb nestled in the heart of Halls Gap. I built the website myself using WordPress, and integrated it with tools like Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to boost visibility and track performance. I handle: SEO and content strategy Guest communication Booking management Coordination with cleaners and support staff This project blends my love of travel, design, and data-driven marketing — and it’s been incredibly rewarding to see it grow.

I have hands-on experience with SEO best practices focused on content optimisation. While I’m not a technical SEO specialist, I understand how to collaborate with developers or use guidance to ensure accessibility and performance are also considered.
